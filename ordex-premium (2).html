How quickly can I integrate ORDEX?
Most developers are up and running within a few hours. Our SDKs handle all the complexity, and our documentation includes copy-paste examples for common use cases. Enterprise integrations typically take 1-2 weeks including testing.
Which carriers do you support?
We support 120+ carriers worldwide including FedEx, UPS, DHL, USPS, and dozens of regional carriers. Our automatic carrier selection chooses the best option based on your criteria.
What's your uptime guarantee?
We guarantee 99.9% uptime SLA for all paid plans. Our infrastructure is distributed across multiple regions with automatic failover. Enterprise plans include a 99.99% SLA with dedicated support.
Can I use ORDEX for international shipping?
Absolutely! ORDEX supports international shipping to 200+ countries. We handle customs documentation, duties calculation, and compliance requirements automatically.
How does pricing work?
We offer simple per-shipment pricing with no hidden fees. Start free with 1,000 shipments/month, then scale as you grow. Volume discounts are available for high-volume shippers.
